GAME DESCRIPTION

The Great Escape is a hyper-casual, free-to-play game where players control an agile Ape racing through thrilling landscapes. The goal is to collect as many fruits as possible while eliminating enemies to climb the leaderboard.

Dodge obstacles, use power-ups, and compete against others to prove your skills. Unlock unique Apes, customize their appearance, and experience fast-paced action like never before!
